About the Metropolian Atlanta Alumni Chapter

Our Mission

The UAPB-Metropolitan Atlanta Alumni Chapter is organized to support and promote the goals and objectives of the University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff and the UAPB National Alumni Association. Our mission is to foster growth through student and alumni recruitment, increase alumni giving and provide scholarships to deserving students from the Metro Atlanta area enrolled in UAPB.

Our Purpose

The purpose of our Alumni Chapter is to sponsor events which present members the opportunity to network and socialize while continuing to pledge support of our alma mater. We invite sponsorship of student groups, e.g., Concert Choir, Football and Basketball teams. We promote the University through participation in community events, give guidance to student and parent groups, and serve as a focal point for addressing member concerns relating to the Alumni office and University.
